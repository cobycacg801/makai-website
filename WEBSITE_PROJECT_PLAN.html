Project Overview
Goal
Build a premium, unique, high-level professional website for Makai US Group LLC. Not a simple landing page — a full multi-page experience that reflects the quality and depth of the company's services. Dark navy + white. English/Spanish bilingual.
Tech Stack
Pure HTML/CSS/JS · Hosted on Netlify · Domain: makaiusgroup.com (registered on Squarespace) · No frameworks needed — full control, fast loading.
Design Direction
Dark navy backgrounds · White typography · Blue accents (#3b82f6, #818cf8) · No gold · Premium feel similar to top-tier tech/finance companies · Subtle animations · Clean sections with strong whitespace.
Key Differentiators
AI-powered chat ("Kai") for lead qualification · Bilingual EN/ES toggle · Deep service pages with real project experience · Integrated MBI pricing and demo CTA.

Kai — AI Chat System
What is Kai?
A Claude-powered AI assistant embedded as a chat widget on makaiusgroup.com. Not a generic bot — a smart, professional assistant that knows Makai's services deeply and converses naturally with potential clients before handing off to Camilo.
Kai's Personality
Professional but warm. Speaks in complete sentences. Never says "I am an AI" unless directly asked. Asks clarifying questions. Available in English and Spanish. Name: Kai (short for Makai).
What Kai Can Do
· Answer questions about all 4 services
· Qualify leads (what type of project, budget, timeline)
· Explain MBI pricing and features
· Book a demo (collect name + email)
· Route to correct service area
· Respond in English or Spanish automatically
· Hand off to Camilo when ready
Status
Planned — to be built after website pages are complete. Will use Claude API as the backend with a custom system prompt defining Kai's personality and knowledge base.
